About

I am a third year Mathematics PhD student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Thiruvananthapuram. My research advisor is Dr. Md. Ramiz Reza. I am originally from Rourkela, Odisha.

I completed my bachelor's at Loyola College, Chennai, and my master's at IISER Thiruvananthapuram.

Apart from math, I am interested in free software, and I enjoy playing badminton and the guitar.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in functional analysis, specifically in the interaction between complex function theory and operator theory. In particular, I study Hilbert spaces of analytic functions with reproducing kernels, especially Dirichlet-type spaces and de Branges–Rovnyak spaces.
